🎶 Request a song

Pick a song, send it up the mountain. Every track below is the real studio library — exported straight from the playout system behind 106.5 FM, 780 AM, and the stream — and your submission is logged for the studio. We don’t promise it airs — the playout system stays untouched — we promise it’s seen. What Sedona asks for shapes what Sedona hears.

📜 The jukebox wall

What the mountain’s been asking for lately — every card a real request from a real listener.

One request per listener every 15 minutes. Requests go to the studio, not into an automatic queue — the playlist stays the station’s call, but every single request gets read. That’s not a loophole; that’s radio. And as songs play, tap the pulse on the player — every vote lands on the studio’s nudge sheet and quietly tunes the suggestions you see here.
