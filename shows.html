Your day on the dial

Mellow Mountain Music opens the day with smooth yacht rock and the local headlines, and the easy sound carries through midday. At 3:00 the World Famous 3 O'Clock Three Pack puts three themed tracks back-to-back, then the Sports & Music Block brings Arizona's teams to the evening.

After 10pm, Coast to Coast AM with George Noory takes over for late-night talk, before Overnight Mellow Mountain Music eases you toward morning. Times shown in Mountain Standard Time.
